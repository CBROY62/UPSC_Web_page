the BPSC 69th Main Exam 2023 on January 3, 2024. The General Hindi paper,

the examination

BPSC 69th Main Exam Date and Time 2023


The BPSC 69th Mains Exam 2023 held from January 3–6, 2024, and January 20–21, 2024.  The exam
consists of Five subjects

  • General Hindi,
  • General Studies First Paper,
  • General Studies Second Paper,
  • Essay, and
  • Optional Paper

    • Each paper will be three hours long, from 11 AM–2 PM. The BPSC released the exam schedule
    for the BPSC 69th CCE Mains 2023 on their official website on December 16, 2023. The BPSC
    Mains Exam Pattern has five papers: General Studies Paper I, General Studies Paper II,
    General Hindi, Essay, and an Optional Paper. The Optional Paper will have MCQ type of
    questions. The duration of the Mains Exam will be 3 hours
